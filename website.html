Giant Panda

Description of Giant Pandas

Giant pandas have a distinctive black and white coat, with black fur around their eyes and on their ears, muzzle, legs and shoulders.

They are 4 to 5 feet tall and weigh up to 300 lbs, according to the National Geographic.

Habitat

In the wild, giant pandas are only found in the remote, mountainous regions of central China, in Sichuan, Shaanxi and Gansu provinces, according to the National Zoo.

Fun Fact

Giant Pandas are no longer endangered species!!
